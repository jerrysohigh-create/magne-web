1. Worldview & Vision

At the end of time, the old gods' order collapses. Light and darkness are reborn in chaos, and the relics of the gods are scattered across the world. Those who obtain the protection of these relics can transcend fate and challenge the gods.

Clash of the Titans tells the epic story of human warriors reclaiming divine power. Players become "chosen heroes," rebuilding order in the ruins of the divine realm, collecting relics, forging equipment, inscribing runes, conquering labyrinths and arenas — gradually awakening their own divine powers.

🎯 Core Philosophy

Gameplay mechanics and in-chain interactions are designed to support immersive strategic engagement within the ecosystem. Clash of the Titans is not just a strategy battle game — it's a dynamically balanced growth ecosystem. Every battle, refinement, and decision shapes the game's resource flow and economic balance.

Core Principles

  • Ownership: Players own all assets and growth outcomes
  • Engagement: Combat and growth form a positive cycle
  • Balance: Consumption and recycling maintain resource health

2. Gameplay System

⚔️ Combat System

Turn-based combat. Higher speed acts first. If no winner after 30 rounds, the attacker loses.

💎 Relic System

Relics are designed to interact with the in-game economy, including potential MHA-based purchase or access mechanisms, subject to final game design and official launch terms. Relics determine a character's core abilities, determined by Divine Power and Quality Coefficient:

Attribute = Base Attribute × (Divine Power ÷ 100) × Quality Coefficient

🔬 Refine System

When Divine Power ≤ 160, players can use PRIMORDIUM (PRI) for refinement:

LevelDivine PowerPRI Cost
Basic125–1403 PRI
Intermediate141–1509 PRI
Advanced151–16015 PRI

Refinement has a fixed probability distribution — higher Divine Power means lower probability. This mechanism is the main pathway for PRI consumption and relic value growth.

⚡ Enhance System

Enhancement is performed using Enhancement Stones. Higher enhancement levels require more stones but provide stable attribute boosts. Success rate decreases as enhancement level increases.
