What Makes MAGNE Different

Not just another smartphone — a device designed from the ground up for the next generation of digital ownership.

為擁有而設計，不只是應用程式

Most phones are optimized for content consumption. MAGNE is optimized for digital asset ownership — with security architecture designed in from the start, not layered on top.

Private AI, on-device

The NPU supports privacy-sensitive on-device AI workflows, reducing reliance on cloud processing for selected use cases.

Web3 is native, not an app

Wallet, swap, stake, bridge — these aren't separate apps you download. They're integrated into the device experience through a secure Web3 space.

離線復原功能

NFC-based encrypted backup means your assets can be recovered even without cloud access. Physical security for digital assets.
